Our AI Architecture

Multi-agent AI system trained on 500K+ patient records, designed to understand the complete patient story ‚Äî medical, social, and emotional.

Agent 1: Narrative Generator

Extracts clinical signals from EHR data and generates first-person patient narratives. Transforms medical jargon into language patients understand.

Agent 2: Diagnostic Reasoner

Performs differential diagnosis analysis with chain-of-thought reasoning. Explains the "why" behind clinical assessments for both patients and providers.

Agent 3: Fact Validator

Cross-references AI outputs against policy documents and clinical guidelines. Eliminates hallucinations through real-time fact-checking and web verification.

Agent 4: Adaptive Questioner

Generates dynamic, context-aware patient questionnaires. Learns from each response to capture social determinants, personal concerns, and lifestyle factors physicians miss.

Result: Holistic patient profiles that capture medical history, social determinants, and personal health context ‚Äî giving physicians the full picture they need, and patients the clarity they deserve.
