How the Algorithm Matches

Our end-to-end AI pipeline — productionized in 2024 — turns complex patient and policy data into precise, explainable matches in real time.

1. Data Ingestion & ETL

Automated Python + SQL pipelines on AWS process 200K+ patient records daily with validation, checkpoints, and diagnostics for 40% higher quality.

2. Pretraining & Feature Learning

Custom PyTorch Transformer learns rich patient-history embeddings via self-attention — enabling deep understanding of medical profiles.

3. Fine-Tuning & Alignment

Multi-objective post-training with focal loss and patient importance weighting prioritizes rare/complex cases for 25% better accuracy.

4. Mixture of Experts Routing

Routes patients to specialized "expert" models by cohort (e.g., chronic conditions) across parallel compute — 22% faster inference.

5. RAG + Re-Ranking

Vector retrieval over 50K+ policies + transformer re-ranking ensures accurate, hallucination-free plan rules — 94% fewer support escalations.

6. Final Matching & Output

Delivers ranked plans with cost-coverage trade-offs, filtering, and rule-based explanations — reducing mismatches by 31% in production.
